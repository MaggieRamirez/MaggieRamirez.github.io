Urban Planning

I'm at graduate student at UNM's Department of Community & Regional Planning School and a transportation and land use planner at an Albuquerque-based civil engineering firm.

I have experience with transportation master plans, housing needs assessments, tribal planning, and bicycle and pedestrian planning. I'm a member of the Urban Land Institute, and I'm also a certified proposal manager. Previously, I worked as a human rights advocate and foreign policy analyst based in Bogota, Colombia.

Writing

My journalism has been published in NACLA, OtherWords, Eurasia Review, and the VC Reporter, and I've written more than 600 published scripts for public radio's The Writer's Almanac.
